What are the Sustainable Development Goals?

The United Nations (UN) is an international organization that intends to solve issues that prevent humanity from progressing. They make decisions and take action to reach 17 global goals, which are known as the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). These goals are topics that the UN believes the world must raise awareness of.

What is Goal #9 and Why is it Important?

SDG #9 is Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure. This goal encourages everyone to be innovative and propose new and useful inventions. It intends to help spread the importance of industrial stability and infrastructural efficiency.

Raising awareness of this goal is also a key to recovering from the pandemic:

  • Provide new solutions to the pandemic
  • Advance infrastructure to support sustainable living and follow COVID-19 guidelines
  • Help industries that are falling due to the pandemic
